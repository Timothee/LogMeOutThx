What is LogMeOut?

LogMeOut is a little script that logs you out of the site you're currently visiting. (or dies trying)

How do I use LogMeOut?

Drag the link on the left to your bookmark bar. Whenever you want to log out of the site you're visiting, click the bookmarklet, or even better, press Cmd+1 (if it's the first bookmark in the bar. YMMV)

Ok, but why wouldn't I just click on the "log out" link on the site?

Excellent question! Well, LogMeOut is mostly for people who use keyboard shortcuts and don't like having to grab the mouse to log out of a site. (call them lazy, call them efficient…) Plus, sometimes the link is inside a menu, meaning two clicks! And sometimes you might not even know where it is…
